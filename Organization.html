# Organization

# How is 0xMENA DAO structured?

At its core, 0xMENA DAO will be member-managed and rely on a DApp and related smart contracts to facilitate investments into projects using digital assets.

# Can members vary the operating agreement or the ways in which 0xMENA DAO operates?

The terms and provisions of the operating agreement may be amended if 50% or more of 0xMENA DAO members, or their delegates, vote to approve the amendment. Voting will be based on the number of 0xMENA DAO Units held by the members at the time of the vote.

# What are the tax issues with 0xMENA DAO?

Each member of 0xMENA DAO must rely on the member's own tax and legal representatives as to the tax consequences of joining 0xMENA DAO or making investments through 0xMENA DAO.
