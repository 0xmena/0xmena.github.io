# What is 0xMENA DAO?

One of the most promising applications of Ethereum is the decentralized (autonomous) organization, also known as a DAO. In 2016, the Ethereum community saw the first great experiment with DAOs with the launch of "The DAO." The DAO raised over $150 million dollars.

0xMENA was organized in the spirit of The DAO, as a member-directed venture capital fund.

Like The DAO, 0xMENA DAO allows Members to pool capital, invest in projects, and share in any proceeds from the investment.

0xMENA DAO is primarily administered via an online application (a "DApp") and related smart contracts.

# 0xMENA is the First DAO in the Middle East and North Africa

We’re on a mission to uplift creators, artists and creatives through the power of DAOs and web3 communities in the MENA region

0xMENA DAO aims to build, support and invest in emerging artists, talented creators and collaborative teams, giving them voice and helping them create successful projects in the metaverse

# Vision

To accelerate MENA’s advancements in the metaverse and become the ultimate launchpad for MENA based projects in the realms of the metaverse

# Mission

Build community, instill decentralized governance, create a treasury that can be used by the strongest most innovative and diversified community in the region, and make a positive impact in the communities where we live and work
